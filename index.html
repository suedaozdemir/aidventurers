Uganda 2025: Wells, Cataract Surgeries & Livelihood Kits

July 2025 by Aidventurers Team

In partnership with local leaders in Kampala and Jinja, our student team delivered 12 water wells, funded 30 cataract surgeries, and provided 100+ goats & bicycles to families. Funds were raised through community events in Dallas.

  • Healthcare: mobile clinics and surgery funds
  • Water: community wells with maintenance training
  • Livelihoods: goats + bicycles for income and mobility
